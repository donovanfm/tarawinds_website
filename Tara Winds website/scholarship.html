Tara Winds Scholarship Fund

The Tara Winds Scholarship Fund was established in 1993 to share the love of music by providing funds to further music education. Since its inception, over $120,000 has been awarded to middle and high school band students to attend summer music camps and colleges.

Scholarships are awarded to middle and high school students as well as graduating seniors pursuing higher education in instrumental music. Recipients are chosen through an application process. Scholarships are recognized and awarded at the Tara Winds Spring Concert.

The Tara Winds Scholarship Fund is funded solely by donations. To donate, click the Donate tab above to access PayPal. Please be sure to specify "Scholarship." Contributions to the Tara Winds Scholarship Fund may be tax deductible. Tax advisors should be consulted for specific details.

In Memory of John Dyson

John Dyson was a longtime supporter of Tara Winds. He served in many behind the scenes roles including serving as the Chairman of the Board of Directors for Tara Winds for seven years. He was a fierce champion of the Tara Winds Scholarship Foundation and its mission to share the love of music by providing scholarships to further music education. John passed away shortly after our Scholarship Concert last year. In lieu of flowers, he had requested donations be made towards the Scholarship Fund. Many of his family and friends honored him in that way. Tara Winds would like to express sincere gratitude for your generosity and kindness in honor of our friend, John Dyson.

About Tara Winds

Tara Winds is an all-adult symphonic band celebrating over 30 years of excellence in musical performance. Our founders, Larry Volman, Ed Davis, and David Gregory envisioned Tara Winds to be a performance medium for local musicians and to encourage school band members to continue performing after completion of their formal studies.
